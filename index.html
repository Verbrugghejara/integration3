This is
Christophe Plantin

He was a master printer who founded one of the most important print houses in Antwerp. His Plantin-Moretus House showcases the world’s oldest printing presses, rare manuscripts, and beautifully preserved interiors.

The map below map highlights key locations with pins. Discover where he went and what he did at each place.

First atlas

The image you see is from the Atlas Ortelius, is the first “modern” world atlas. The Antwerp mapmaker Abraham Ortelius (1527-1598) had a number of existing maps redrawn in a standard format and published them as a book. It was first published in 1570 at Christophe Plantin's printing house.
